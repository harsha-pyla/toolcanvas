How is a Student / Fresher Resume Structured for ATS Checks?

Unlike resumes for candidates with work experience, a fresher's resume needs to prioritize **academic credentials, developer skills, and technical projects**. Because freshers have limited professional history, Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan for matching course keywords and project bullet achievements to measure qualifications.

Our templates place **Education** and **Academic Projects** at the top. The clean, single-column HTML structures compile into selectable text layers when downloaded via system print engines (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P). This guarantees that text parses accurately on recruiter databases and scoring algorithms with no lost or scrambled font layouts.

Formatting Guidelines for Recent Graduate Resumes

To maximize your ATS score and create a professional first impression, each of our 5 layouts implements standard resume architecture:

  • Standard Headers: Section titles are named clearly (e.g., "Education", "Projects", "Skills") ensuring parsing bots index data in correct sections.
  • Focus on Projects: Dedicates larger content blocks to detail GitHub systems, academic research, and hackathon prototypes using action verbs.
  • Linear Layouts: Uses a single-column sequence that reads top-to-bottom, avoiding tabular structures that break standard reading parsers.
  • No Icons or Graphics: Eliminates progress sliders or graphics, representing skill proficiency cleanly as plain text tags.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my resume template as a PDF?

Click the "See & Download" button on any card. Once the preview loads, hit the blue **"Download PDF"** button (or Ctrl+P). Set the print destination to **"Save as PDF"**, check "Enable background graphics" to capture styles, and hit Save.

Why is there pre-filled data on the templates?

The templates are pre-filled with a high-quality recent computer science graduate resume profile. This serves as a formatting model for structuring your own degree courses, side projects, skills list, and leadership roles.

Will my resume fit on a single page?

Yes. All templates are designed with compact line-heights and margins tailored to keep a standard student profile packed tightly on a single sheet of A4/Letter paper when saved.
