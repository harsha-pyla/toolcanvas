Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit ToolCanvas, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it safe. We've kept it straightforward — no legal jargon where we can avoid it.

Information We Collect

Most of our tools process all data directly on your device, which means the data you work with (images, text, links, etc.) stays private. We don't upload your files to our servers, and we don't store any personal data from the tools you use.

That said, we do collect some basic, non-personal information automatically when you visit our site:

  • Usage data: Pages you visit, how long you spend on a page, and which tools you use. This helps us understand what's working and what needs improvement.
  • Device information: Your browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and general geographic region (country/city level, not your exact location).
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device that help us analyze traffic and serve relevant content. More on this below.

If you reach out to us through our contact form, we'll receive your name, email address, and message. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use the non-personal information we collect to:

  • Understand how people use our tools so we can make them better.
  • Identify and fix technical issues or bugs.
  • Analyze traffic patterns to guide decisions about which tools to build next.
  • Serve relevant advertisements through our ad partners.

We don't sell your information to anyone. Period.

Cookies

ToolCanvas uses cookies — small text files placed on your device — to help us understand site traffic and improve your experience. Here's what you should know:

  • Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics to track how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect information like which pages you visit and how long you stay. Google Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized, so we can't identify you personally from it.
  • Advertising cookies: We use Google AdSense to display ads on our site. AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history across different websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Most browsers let you control or block cookies through their settings. Keep in mind that disabling cookies may affect how some parts of the site work.

Third-Party Services

We work with a small number of trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how people use our site. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google AdSense: Serves advertisements on our site to help keep our tools free. Google may collect and use data for ad personalization. You can learn more about how Google uses your data at Google's partner sites page.

These services operate under their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them if you'd like to understand how they handle your data.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information associated with our site. Our website is served over HTTPS, which encrypts data transmitted between your browser and our servers. Since our tools process data locally in your browser, there's minimal risk of your personal content being exposed through our platform.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security. We do our best, but we want to be upfront about that.

Children's Privacy

ToolCanvas is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you're a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll take steps to remove that information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time as we add new tools, adopt new technologies, or when regulations change. When we make changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to check back occasionally so you're aware of any updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, feel free to reach out through our contact page. We're happy to help.
