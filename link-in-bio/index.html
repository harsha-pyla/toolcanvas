How to Use the Link in Bio Generator

Getting your custom bio page ready takes less than a minute. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter your profile details. Choose a username, write a bio of up to 40 characters, and upload a profile photo (or paste an image URL).
  2. Add your links. Click "Add Link" to add up to 10 clickable links. Enter a title and the URL.
  3. Choose a theme. Select from Sunshine Pop, Soft Mist, Electric Lime, Editorial Cream, Salsa Red, Cyber Magenta, or configure your own Custom Colors.
  4. Generate and Share. Hit "Generate Link" to get a direct URL you can copy immediately, or click "Download HTML File" to host it yourself.

What Is a Link in Bio?

A "link in bio" page is a simple landing page that collects all your important links in one place. It's most commonly used on social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter, where you only get one clickable link in your profile. Instead of linking to a single website, you point followers to your bio page, which gives them easy access to everything — your shop, your latest video, a newsletter signup, your other social accounts, and more.

Services like Linktree and Beacons made this concept popular, but those come with branding, limits, or paid tiers. With this generator, you own the HTML file outright. There's no third-party branding, no tracking, and no monthly fee. You get a clean, self-contained page that you can host yourself for free.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Completely free, no catch. The tool runs entirely in your browser. We don't store your data, and the HTML file you download is yours to keep and host however you like. There's no account to create and no premium tier.

Where can I host my bio page?

Anywhere that serves static HTML files. Popular free options include GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, and Cloudflare Pages. You could also upload it to your own web hosting. Since it's a single HTML file with everything inlined, there's nothing else to configure.

Can I edit the page after downloading it?

Absolutely. The downloaded file is standard web code. Open it in any text editor or code editor to make changes. You can also come back here, rebuild the page with your updates, and download a fresh copy.

Will my profile image work in the shareable link?

Because ToolCanvas runs 100% in your browser without saving files on a database server, uploaded images cannot be encoded inside the generated shareable link. If you want to use the shareable link feature, please paste an image URL hosted publicly (e.g. on Imgur or Gravatar) in the URL field instead. In contrast, if you download the standalone HTML file, uploaded photos are fully supported and embedded directly in the file!
